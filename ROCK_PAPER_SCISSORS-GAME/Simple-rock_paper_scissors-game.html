About the Game
Rock-Paper-Scissors is a timeless hand game played between two people, where each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand: rock, paper, or scissors. It's simple, fun, and widely recognized across the globe. In this digital version of the classic game, players can compete against the computer, testing their strategy and luck. The rules remain the same: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. Keep track of your wins, losses, and ties as you play, and see if you can beat the machine.
